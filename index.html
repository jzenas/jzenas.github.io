My mission

Beyond my technical experience, I am a very inquisitive and growth focused individual. Particularly, I believe that it is important to learn about fields outside of computer science in order to make a widespread impact. During my times as a waitress at Texas Roadhouse and a sales associate at Lands' End, I clearly saw how technological innovations could be used to improve the lives of workers, yet there has been hardly any changes in the technologies that restaurants and stores use in decades. I have seen first had how eye-opening it can be to look into different industries and see the untapped potential of technology. As a result, I want to be a changing force in the field of computer science and bring improvements to aspects of our daily lives that are typically overlooked by technical professionals. I aspire to think outside of the box, and create positive changes where others fail to look.
